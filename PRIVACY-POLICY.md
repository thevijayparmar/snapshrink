# Privacy Policy — Snapshrink

**Last updated: July 2026**
**Developer: Vijay Parmar**
**GitHub: https://github.com/thevijayparmar/snapshrink**

---

## Summary (Plain English)

Snapshrink does **not collect, transmit, or store any personal data**. Your images never leave your computer. The app runs entirely offline on your local machine.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Snapshrink collects **no data of any kind**. Specifically:

- No images or files you convert are uploaded anywhere
- No usage statistics or telemetry are collected
- No crash reports are sent
- No analytics, tracking, or logging of any kind
- No user accounts, sign-ups, or logins required

---

## 2. How the App Works

All image processing — converting, resizing, and compressing — happens **locally on your own computer**. Your original files are never modified (Snapshrink always saves output as a new file). No data is ever sent over the internet.

---

## 3. Internet Access

Snapshrink does **not connect to the internet**. The app requires no network access to function. The only network-related activity is:

- The Windows installer (`SnapshrinkSetup.exe`) may be downloaded from GitHub Releases or the Microsoft Store — this is standard installer delivery, not data collection.

---

## 4. Local Storage

Snapshrink stores one configuration file (`config.json`) in your application folder. This file contains only your personal app preferences (e.g., preferred output format, quality settings). It stays on your machine and is never transmitted anywhere.

---

## 5. Registry

Snapshrink writes a small number of Windows Registry entries to:

- Register the right-click context menu ("Convert with Snapshrink", etc.)
- Set up the app to auto-start its background daemon with Windows (if enabled)

These entries are standard Windows integration mechanisms. They contain no personal data and are fully removed when you uninstall the app.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Snapshrink uses **no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or cloud services** of any kind.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Snapshrink does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no special handling required.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds an optional cloud feature), the updated policy will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Significant changes will also be noted in the release notes.
