## Full description

Snapshrink adds a fast, no-nonsense image converter directly to your
Windows right-click menu. No need to open an editor, guess at a quality
slider, or manage a "Save As" dialog for every single file.

**What you can do:**
• Convert any image to JPG, PNG, or WebP in one click
• Resize to an exact pixel limit (250px up to 2500px)
• Compress to an exact file size limit (10 KB up to 1 MB) — Snapshrink
  finds the right quality automatically, no trial and error
• Use the Ctrl+Alt+J hotkey for instant conversion without opening any
  window at all
• Batch-process an entire folder of images at once
• Strip EXIF/GPS metadata with a single toggle, for privacy

**Why people use it:**
Upload forms that reject files over a certain size. Photos that arrive as
HEIC, WebP, or BMP when you just need a plain JPG. Job portals and
government forms with strict pixel-size requirements. Snapshrink handles
all of this without overwriting your original files or forcing you
through repeated manual exports.

**Supported formats:** JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, TIFF, BMP, ICO, HEIC/HEIF

Snapshrink is completely free, with no ads, no trial period, and no
"pro" upsell. It's open source — the full code is available on GitHub
for anyone to inspect, audit, or contribute to.
